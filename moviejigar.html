WHAT TO KNOW

Jigarthanda DoubleX is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language action drama film[1] written and directed by Karthik Subbaraj and jointly produced by him, Kaarthekeyen Santhanam, S. Kathiresan and Alankar Pandian under Stone Bench Films, Five Star Creations and Invenio Origin.

The film is a spiritual successor and prequel to Jigarthanda (2014) and stars Raghava Lawrence, S. J. Suryah and Nimisha Sajayan. Set in the 1970s, it revolves around an upcoming policeman trying to kill a gangster by going undercover as a filmmaker.

Jigarthanda DoubleX was released on 10 November 2023, the week of Diwali, to critical acclaim, with praise directed towards its direction, script, cast performances, music, cinematography, production values, editing, emotional depth, and action sequences. Many critics consider the sequel superior to the first.
